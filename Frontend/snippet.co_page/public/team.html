About Snippet.co

Founded with a mission to merge technology with creativity, Snippet.co is a forward-thinking software company specializing in AI-driven music solutions. We believe that music is a universal language, and our goal is to make it more accessible, personalized, and meaningful for everyone.

Our team combines expertise in machine learning, software engineering, design, and music curation to build products that resonate with millions of users worldwide.

Our Product: Beatify

Beatify is our flagship AI-powered music recommendation platform that transforms how people discover and enjoy music. With over 100 million tracks, intelligent playlists, and personalized recommendations, Beatify learns your taste and mood to deliver the perfect soundtrack for every moment.

Built on cutting-edge machine learning algorithms and designed with a user-first philosophy, Beatify offers high-quality streaming, offline listening, live radio, and seamless cross-platform synchronization—all wrapped in a beautiful, intuitive interface.
