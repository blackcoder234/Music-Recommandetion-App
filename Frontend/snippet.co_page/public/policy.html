Privacy Policy

At Snippet.co, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use Beatify.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. The data we collect falls into the following categories:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password, date of birth, and profile picture when you create an account
  • Payment Information: Billing details, payment card information (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
  • User Content: Playlists you create, songs you like, comments, and other content you share
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting customer support

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Songs played, listening history, search queries, playlists accessed, skipped tracks, and playback duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, mobile network information, and unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (precise location only with your permission)
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: If you connect via Google, Apple, or Facebook, we receive basic profile information
  • Partners: Data from business partners and service providers who help us deliver our services

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Description
Service Delivery Provide, maintain, and improve Beatify's core functionality
Personalization Create AI-powered recommendations and personalized playlists
Communication Send service updates, promotional emails, and respond to inquiries
Account Management Manage subscriptions, billing, and authentication
Analytics Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
Security Detect fraud, prevent abuse, and ensure platform safety
Legal Compliance Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

AI-Powered Recommendations: We use machine learning algorithms to analyze your listening habits and preferences to suggest music you'll love. This processing happens securely and helps create your personalized Beatify experience.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate Beatify, including:

  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
  • Customer support tools (Zendesk, Intercom)
  • Email service providers (SendGrid, Mailchimp)

3.2 Business Transfers

If Snippet.co is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations, court orders, or subpoenas
  • Protect Snippet.co's rights and property
  • Investigate fraud or security issues
  • Protect the safety of users or the public

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly consent, such as when sharing playlists on social media.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage data.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality (login, security)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze site performance and usage patterns
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads (only for free tier users)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit some features of Beatify.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

5.1 Access & Portability

Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

5.2 Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings.

5.3 Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated data. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes.

5.4 Opt-Out

Unsubscribe from marketing emails, disable personalized ads, or limit data collection.

5.5 Restriction

Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@snippet.co or visit your account settings. We'll respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access policies and role-based permissions
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Data centers with physical and digital security measures
  • Incident Response: Protocols to detect and respond to security breaches

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. If a breach occurs, we'll notify affected users as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Beatify is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we've collected data from a child under 13, we'll delete it immediately.

For users aged 13-17, parental consent may be required in certain jurisdictions. Please check your local laws.

8. International Data Transfers

Beatify operates globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States, where data protection laws may differ.

We use appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and Privacy Shield frameworks (where applicable), to protect your data during international transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you via:

  • Email to your registered address
  • In-app notification
  • Prominent notice on our website

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. Your continued use of Beatify after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@snippet.co
  • Support: Visit our Support Center
  • Address: Snippet.co, San Francisco, CA 94103, USA

For EU residents: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

Need Help Understanding Our Policy?

Our support team is here to answer any questions about how we handle your data.
