Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to Account > Subscription > Cancel Plan. You will continue to have access to Premium features until the end of your billing cycle.
Can I listen to music offline?
Yes! With Beatify Premium, you can download albums, playlists, and podcasts to your device and listen without an internet connection.
How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. We'll send a reset link to your registered email address.
Is there a family plan?
Yes, the Beatify Premium Family plan allows up to 6 distinct accounts for family members living at the same address.
