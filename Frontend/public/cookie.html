Cookie Policy

At Beatify, we believe in transparency. This policy explains how we use cookies to ensure you get the best listening experience possible.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small strings of text that a website sends to your device's browser. The browser saves this information and relays it back to the website's server when you revisit. We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in.
  • Remember your player volume and quality settings.
  • Understand how you use our app to improve features.

2. Recent Updates (2025/2026)

To comply with the latest global standards (GDPR, CCPA, and ePrivacy), we have updated our cookie handling. We now categorize cookies strictly into Essential, Functional, Analytics, and Targeting groups. You have granual control over everything except "Essential" cookies, which are required for the site to work.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Necessary for technical operations (e.g., Playing music, Logging in). You cannot opt-out of these.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand traffic patterns. We use anonymized data to see which specific tracks or genres are trending.

4. Managing Your Cookies

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the button at the top of this page. Additionally, most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org.
